Commentary:
Thursday, February 28, 2008: BONUS COMIC

This commentary was written in December 2024.

...So you thought the prior comic was the last one, right? Well, originally it was! For some context, check out the foreword. Ok, done? Right.

So the truth is that, after I did my original run of Official Comics, I kept drawing and making comics for a number of years - I just didn't upload them to Comic Genesis is all. Instead, I posted them on Flickr. Remember Flickr?

You can see I wasn't doing digital comics by this point in 2008. Instead, I was using colored markers for everything. I had some difficulty controlling stroke width, but I think the comics I drew from this era otherwise looked really good.

But also, I think this comic is the single best visual gag I ever wrote. I could be wrong about that - I seem to recall that people I showed this to didn't really get it. But personally, I think it's great.

--Josh
