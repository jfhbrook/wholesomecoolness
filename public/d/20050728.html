Commentary:
Thursday , July 28 , 2005: And we're BACK! (Like Jimmy Fallon said it when he was that DJ on SNL)

Wow... so I'm back from Science Camp. There isn't much to say, except:

WOO! I <3 SCIENCE CAMP!!!

Anyhow, so that's that. Oh, I also got some goodies, both at camp and after I got home. I got an assload of Mardi Gras beads from Devin (he's from New Orleans), "Understanding Comics" from Ma and Pa, case lights from my bro-bro, a couple music CD's and some Escher stuff. That, and a few bumper stickers and t-shirts. Also, about a hundred new friends. :P

Pretty soon, I'm gonna start drawing again. It will be nice. I even have some ideas in mind...well, okay, one, but whatever.

Las Vegas was also pretty neat, but I preferred the Grand Canyon and Laughlin. Las Vegas is just so... commercial, you know? It's phony in a Holden Caulfield sort of way.

I'll probably have more to say this weekend, but for now I'll just finish with what I originally wrote:

I still like this page. Some of them bother me, but this one's still pretty nifty I think. I like the circle of frames. The only bothersome thing is the red around the text at the bottom.

And how bout that choice of font? Silkscreen is pretty common for sprite comics, but... I dunno. It works. What do you think?

--Joshy-Josh*
