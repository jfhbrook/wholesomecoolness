I think I'll rant about a question that will VERY likely make it into my FAQs if I ever compile one. Well, not really a question, but more of a statement, or a request: Variations of "Dude! You should make a comic about that!!". I **HATE** this question. I only like to draw comics about things I want to draw them about, and I hate requests. I feel bad, though, because the question's usually pretty innocent. Still, for those that read, please don't suggest comic ideas. I don't mind, of course, a "Hey, this is funny, I thought you might get a kick out of this", but please no "OMG!!! You should draw a COMIC about this LOLOMG!!!1".

You get the idea.
